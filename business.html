Business Practices

Mongolia's business practices are constantly evolving with modernization, preserving traditional nomadic economic characteristics while integrating modern business models.

Animal Husbandry

Animal husbandry is the traditional economic backbone of Mongolia, mainly including horse breeding, cattle raising, and sheep farming. Mongolian animal products such as wool, dairy, and meat have a certain competitiveness in the international market.

Mining Industry

Mongolia has rich mineral resources, including coal, copper, and gold, which are important export commodities. The development of the mining industry has brought significant economic benefits to Mongolia.

Tourism

With the rise of global tourism, Mongolia's unique natural landscapes and cultural heritage attract an increasing number of international tourists. Tourism has become an important part of Mongolia's economy.
