History

Mongolia's history is long and filled with legends. From Genghis Khan uniting the Mongol tribes and establishing the Mongol Empire to the Yuan Dynasty and modern transformations, every page of Mongolian history is brimming with heroism and grandeur.

Genghis Khan Era

In 1206, Genghis Khan unified the Mongol tribes, creating the Mongol Empire, which became the largest contiguous empire in history. Genghis Khan's military strategy and leadership skills enabled the rapid expansion of the empire across vast regions of Eurasia.

Yuan Dynasty Era

In 1271, Kublai Khan established the Yuan Dynasty, becoming a significant period in Chinese history. The establishment of the Yuan Dynasty facilitated cultural exchange and trade between East and West.

Modern Mongolia

In the early 20th century, Mongolia gained independence from the Qing Dynasty and established the modern state of Mongolia. In 1992, Mongolia became a democratic country through constitutional reforms.
